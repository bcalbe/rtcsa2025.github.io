Call for Posters and Demos

The RTCSA 2025 Posters and Demos session provides an opportunity to present early-stage work and receive feedback from both the real-time/embedded systems community and the non-volatile memory systems community from NVMSA, which is co-located with the conference. We invite both research and industry presentations in all areas listed in the call for papers.

 

Abstract Submission Guidelines

  • Poster/demo abstracts have at most 2 pages of technical content, including all text, figures, tables, appendices, references, etc.
  • The submitted extended abstracts are subject to single-blind reviews.
  • Authors should use an IEEE two-column conference style with 10pt font size, on US Letter paper size according to the IEEE conference paper proceedings format.
  • Please add a "Poster:" or "Demo:" prefix to your title.
  • The accepted abstracts will be published in the RTCSA 2025's proceedings.
  • Submission web page at:
    • Submission Portal

Important Dates:

  • Submission deadline: June 19, 2025 AoE
  • Acceptance notification: July 4, 2025
  • Camera-ready deadline: July 11, 2025

Welcome to RTCSA 2025

IEEE RTCSA 2025, the 31st IEEE International Conference on Embedded and Real-Time Computing Systems and Application, will be held in Singapore on August 20-22, 2025. It will be co-located with NVMSA 2025. Accepted papers will be submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore, subject to meeting IEEE Xplore's scope and quality requirements.

Awards

Best Paper Award: Predictable Memory Bandwidth Regulation for DynamIQ Arm Systems
Ashutosh Pradhan, Daniele Ottaviano, Yi Jiang, Haozheng Huang, Jiajia Zhang, Alexander Zuepke, Andrea Bastoni and Marco Caccamo.

Best Students Paper Award: EtherTime: Cross-vendor Evaluation of PTP/NTP on Ethernet-based COTS Embedded Platforms
Vincent Bode, William Shen and Arpan Gujarati.

Outstanding Paper Award: MURAL: A Multi-Resolution Anytime Framework for LiDAR Object Detection Deep Neural Networks
Ahmet Soyyigit, Shuochao Yao and Heechul Yun.

Best Demo Award: VecSim: A Vehicle Edge Computing Simulator for Real-Time Applications
Chuanchao Gao and Arvind Easwaran

Best Poster Award: Reusable Software Components to Prototype and Evaluate Mixed-Criticality Scheduling Policies
Alan le Boudec, Hai Nam Tran, Stéphane Rubini, Alexandre Skrzyniarz and Frank Singhoff

Best Presentation: by Diogo Costa for paper "Beyond the Bermuda Triangle of Contention: IOMMU Interference in Mixed Criticality Systems"

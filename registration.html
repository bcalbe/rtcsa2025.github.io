Cancellation Policy

  • If you have already registered for RTCSA 2025 and find that you are unable to attend the conference, please notify RTCSA 2025 Registration Chair in writing to levanduc@ieee.org to cancel your registration and request a refund if applicable.
  • Cancellation deadline: August 4th, 2025 AoE in terms of the receipt time of the request email.
  • Cancellation fee:
    • 50% of the registration fee before the cancellation deadline;
    • 100% of the registration free after the cancellation deadline (i.e., no refund).
  • All refunds will be made after the symposium.

Admission Types

  • Conference Registration - Author: Registration for all authors (full papers, short papers, posters, and demos) presenting at the conference, with access to the whole conference program, breaks, lunch, reception, and banquet.
  • Conference Registration - Non-Author: Registration for general attendee with access to the whole conference program, breaks, lunch, reception, and banquet.

Author Information/Rules

At least one unique author must register for RTCSA'25 at the full registration rate (i.e., non-student) for the full/short conference paper to appear in the proceedings. To encourage poster/demo presentations, a full registration can cover a full/short conference paper and a demo/poster abstract. For student demo/poster presenter without a conference paper, a student registration can cover a demo/poster abstract. To elaborate:

  • A non-student registration covers one conference paper (either full or short) and/or one poster/demo abstract. For this case, please specify the conference paper ID in the registration system and email the poster/demo abstract ID and title to the Registration Chair Duc Van Le at levanduc@ieee.org.
  • A student registration covers one poster/demo abstract authored by the student registrant.
  • A registration cannot cover a conference paper and/or an extended abstract that aren't/isn't authored by the registrant.

Notes:

  • Each accepted contribution needs to meet the requirement by July 18th 2025. Otherwise, the contribution may be excluded from the conference program and proceedings.
  • Wire transfer payment should be sent 2-3 weeks prior to the deadline of July 18th 2025 to allow sufficient time for the conference to receive payment.
  • Author registrations to meet the above requirement are non-refundable.
  • Life member rates are applicable to an IEEE Member whose membership status is “Life”.
  • RTCSA'25 only accepts in-person presentations. Overseas presenting authors, invited speakers, and panelists should plan the travels and entry visas accordingly. We will allow replacement in-person paper/poster/demo presenters or virtual paper presentations only in case of visa issues, illness, or other genuine hardship and with prior permission of the General Co-Chairs based on documentary proofs. Papers and extended abstracts without presentations will be removed from the conference proceedings.

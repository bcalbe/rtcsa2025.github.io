Cancellation Policy

  • If you have already registered for RTCSA 2025 and find that you are unable to attend the conference, please notify RTCSA 2025 Registration Chair in writing to levanduc@ieee.org to cancel your registration and request a refund if applicable.
  • Cancellation deadline: August 4th, 2025 AoE in terms of the receipt time of the request email.
  • Cancellation fee:
    • 50% of the registration fee before the cancellation deadline;
    • 100% of the registration free after the cancellation deadline (i.e., no refund).
  • All refunds will be made after the symposium.

Admission Types

  • Conference Registration - Author: Registration for all authors (full papers, short papers, posters, and demos) presenting at the conference, with access to the whole conference program, breaks, lunch, reception, and banquet.
  • Conference Registration - Non-Author: Registration for general attendee with access to the whole conference program, breaks, lunch, reception, and banquet.
